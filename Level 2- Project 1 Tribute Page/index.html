About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist and philanthropist who served as the chairman of Tata Sons. Under his leadership, Tata Group expanded globally, launching the Tata Nano and acquiring Jaguar Land Rover. He is known for his ethical business practices, innovation, and philanthropy.
